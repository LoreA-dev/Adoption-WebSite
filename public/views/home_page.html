    Let's adopt

    Take in mind this requirements for the adoption

    You have to be an adult

    It's important to be legal age, because that tells us that the adopter is responsable, and will take care of the animal.

    Have approved form

    Is necessary to know your current condition and environment, knowing if you can take care of the pet. Form has to be approved.

    Enough space for the pet

    They need space at home where they can go to clear their minds, give them their comfort space.

    Sterilization commitment

    The sterilisation is a requirement for the adoption. Sterilisation reduces the number of animals on the street.
